## Design Strategy: Family Gathering PT Persada Cipta Usaha

### 1. Digital Campaign Context

The focus shifts from "Spiritual Longing" to "Corporate Synergy & Joy." This design captures the human side of PT Persada Cipta Usaha, highlighting employee appreciation and family bonds.

### 2. Design Principles (Photo Layout)

* Dynamic Grids: Use a "Hero & Detail" layout. One large group photo paired with 2-3 smaller candid shots (games, kids, food).
* Corporate Branding: Integrate PT Persada Cipta Usaha’s brand colors as borders or overlays to keep it professional.
* Warmth & Energy: Use brighter, saturated tones to evoke happiness, replacing the muted tones of the Umrah campaign.

### 3. Canva Execution (How-To)

* Frames & Grids: Use the "Grids" tool in Elements for perfect alignment. Mix wide rectangles with squares for a modern "scrapbook" feel.
* Unified Filters: Apply a single filter (e.g., "Solar" at 25%) to all photos. This fixes the mismatched lighting from different phone cameras.
* Typography: Use bold, clean fonts like Montserrat for titles: *"Together as One: PCU Family Day."*
* Logo Placement: Place the company logo in the top-right corner with a subtle "Drop Shadow" to make it pop.
