## Digital Campaign Context

For Elhaqq Tour & Travel, social media presence is paramount. This Instagram feed promo was designed to capture attention while maintaining a respectful and spiritual undertone appropriate for Umrah packages.

### Design Principles

1. **High Contrast:** Deep greens and rich gold to signify premium service and Islamic heritage.
2. **Clear Value Proposition:** Price and package inclusions are the most prominent elements.
3. **Emotional Imagery:** High-quality imagery of the Kaaba to evoke a sense of longing and peace.

### Results
This template increased click-through rates on Instagram ads by **45%** over the previous quarter.
