## Capturing Attention in Seconds

Instagram Stories are fleeting — the design must be instantly recognizable and guide the user's eye to the action button.

### Key Elements
- **Vertical Optimization:** Designed exclusively for 9:16 aspect ratio.
- **Negative Space:** Strategic room at top and bottom so Instagram UI never covers critical info.
- **Bold Call-to-Action:** Contrasting accent colors for "Book Now" buttons drive conversions.

Fully modular — the marketing team can swap images and text in minutes for daily updates.
